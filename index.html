I am an Economist with main interests in Development, Trade, Economic Geography and Machine Learning.

In my work, I study how climate related shocks affect the human, social and economic development. In order to do so, I rely upon reduced-form causal inference and/or structural modelling and estimation, using spatial trade theory for the latter.

My job market paper focuses in understanding and quantifying how climate change can lead to migration in sub-Saharan Africa, within and across countries, during the next decades.
